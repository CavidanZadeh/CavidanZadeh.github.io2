Principles of Information Systems Course - Group Project 1 : Logic Gates and Digital Circuits

Image Placeholder

In this group project we studied logic gates and simple digital circuits. Our team members did not know each other before the project, so we first learned how to communicate and divide tasks. We used simple diagrams and small simulations to show how gates work together to make decisions. Working with new team members helped me improve teamwork, planning, and problem solving under time limits.

What I learned: basic digital logic, team communication, task division, and collaborative problem solving.

Principles of Information Systems Course - Group Project 2 : Hour of AI Community Visit

Image Placeholder

For the Hour of AI project our group visited a regional school and taught basic AI ideas to children. We planned short lessons and simple activities so students could try fun examples of how AI works. I helped prepare materials and explained concepts in an easy way. The visit showed me how to present technical ideas for a young audience and how to work with a team to run a live activity.

What I learned: teaching skills, clear communication, event planning, and the value of community outreach.
